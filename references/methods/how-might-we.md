# 🔍 How Might We ?

**Phase:** Discover · **Emoji:** 🔍

### Description

---

How might we (HMW) is an ideation technique used to generate creative solutions to problems. It is a question-asking technique that encourages divergent thinking by asking participants to think beyond the obvious and come up with new and innovative ideas.
