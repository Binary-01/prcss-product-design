# 🔍 Shadowing

**Phase:** Discover · **Emoji:** 🔍

> Verbatim Design Method from the PRCSS* template. The emoji reflects the primary phase this method belongs to.

### Description

---

Shadowing is a technique used to gain insight into how a person interacts with a product or service. It involves observing and recording a person as they use the product or service, typically in their natural environment.

This allows UX designers and researchers to understand the user's thought process, behavior, and emotions as they navigate the product or service, which can help identify areas for improvement. Shadowing is often used in conjunction with other UX research methods, such as interviews and surveys, to gain a more comprehensive understanding of user behavior and needs.
