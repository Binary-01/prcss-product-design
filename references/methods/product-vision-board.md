# 🔍 Product Vision Board

**Phase:** Discover · **Emoji:** 🔍

### Description

---

A product vision board is used by product teams to define and communicate the long-term vision for a product. It typically includes a high-level description of the product, along with its key features, target audience, and potential market.

To use a product vision board, the product team should first clearly define the product's overall vision and goals. This may involve conducting market research, gathering feedback from potential customers, and defining the product's key differentiators. The team can then create a visual representation of the product vision, which may include images, diagrams, and other visual elements that help to convey the product's key features and benefits.

Once the product vision board has been created, it can be used as a reference point for the entire product team, helping to ensure that everyone is working towards the same long-term goals. It can also be shared with stakeholders, such as investors and executives, to help them understand the direction that the product is heading in.
