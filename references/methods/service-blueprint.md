# 🎯 Service Blueprint

**Phase:** Define · **Emoji:** 🎯

> Verbatim Design Method from the PRCSS* template. The emoji reflects the primary phase this method belongs to.

### Description

---

A service blueprint is a diagram that represents the entire service delivery process by listing all the activities that occur at each phase and are performed by the various roles involved. A service blueprint is first created by listing all the actors involved in the service process on the vertical axis and all the steps required to deliver the service on the horizontal axis. The resulting matrix allows us to represent the flow of actions that each role needs to perform during the process, including actions that the user can see (above the line of sight) and actions that take place in the back office (below the line of sight). Roles can be performed by humans or by other types of entities (organizations, departments, artificial intelligence, machines, etc.).
