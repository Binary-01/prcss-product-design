# 🧠 Crazy 8

**Phase:** Ideate · **Emoji:** 🧠

> Verbatim Design Method from the PRCSS* template. The emoji reflects the primary phase this method belongs to.

### Description

---

The Crazy 8 method is a design thinking technique that is used to generate new ideas.

The method is simple: you take a sheet of paper and draw 8 lines that intersect in the middle. Then, you brainstorm 8 ideas that are related to the problem or challenge you are trying to solve.

After that, you vote on the best ideas and narrow it down to the top 3. A workshop to quickly generate ideas.
