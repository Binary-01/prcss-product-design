# 🎯 Hypothesis Canvas

**Phase:** Define · **Emoji:** 🎯

### Description

---

The Hypothesis Canvas is a tool for generating, organizing, and prioritizing business hypotheses. It is a way to structure and document assumptions about why a business problem exists and how it can be solved.

The canvas consists of four quadrants, each representing a key aspect of the problem and potential solution. In the upper left quadrant, the problem is defined and clarified. In the upper right quadrant, the current state of the problem is explored, including the underlying causes and effects. In the lower left quadrant, potential solutions are brainstormed and evaluated. In the lower right quadrant, the most promising solution is selected and a plan is developed to test and validate it.

The Hypothesis Canvas provides a framework for systematically and rigorously thinking through a business problem, and can help teams generate and prioritize ideas more effectively.
