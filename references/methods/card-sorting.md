### Description

---

A card sorting workshop is a method used to help understand how people think about and organize information. Card sorting workshops are used to generate ideas, collect data, and create consensus around a shared understanding of a topic.

The workshop begins with a facilitator asking participants to sort a set of cards, each of which has a piece of information on it, into groups. The facilitator then asks the participants to explain their reasoning for grouping the cards in the way they did.
