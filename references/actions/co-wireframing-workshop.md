### Description

---

Co-wireframing is a collaborative process in which a group of designers and stakeholders work together to create a wireframe, which is a low-fidelity visual representation of a website or application.

A wireframe typically includes the layout of the page, the location of elements such as buttons and menus, and the content that will be included on the page. In a co-wireframing workshop, the goal is to create a wireframe that accurately reflects the goals and needs of the project, and that can be used as a guide for the development of the final product.

This type of workshop typically involves a facilitated discussion among the participants, as well as the use of wireframing tools and techniques to create the wireframe.
