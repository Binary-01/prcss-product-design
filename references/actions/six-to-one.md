### Description

---

The Six to One design methodology is a systematic approach to problem solving that follows six distinct steps:

1. Define the problem
2. Research the problem
3. Brainstorm solutions
4. Evaluate solutions
5. Select the best solution
6. Implement the solution

This methodology helps to ensure that all possible solutions are explored, and that the best solution is chosen. Additionally, it encourages creative thinking and collaboration among team members.
