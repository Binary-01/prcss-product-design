### Description

---

A journey map is a composite representation that describes step-by-step how users interact with your service. The process is mapped from the user's perspective, describing what happens at each stage of the interaction, which touchpoints are involved, and what obstacles and barriers may be encountered. Journey maps are often integrated with an additional layer representing the level of positive/negative emotions experienced during an interaction.
