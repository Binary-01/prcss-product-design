# 🔍 Discover

**Color:** blue · **Emoji:** 🔍

## Intent
Understand users, context, and the real problem before committing to any solution. This is the divergent phase of the first diamond — you are expanding what you know, not narrowing it.

## Canonical methods (shipped with the template)
- **✅ Brief** — document requirements, goals, constraints. See `../methods/brief.md`.
- **Objectives** — define SMART product objectives. See `../methods/objectives.md`.

## Typical methods to add to this phase
- Stakeholder interviews
- User / customer interviews
- Desk research & competitive analysis
- Analytics review (if the product exists)
- Heuristic audit of an existing product
- Field study / contextual inquiry
- Diary studies

## Typical outputs
- A one-pager project brief.
- A raw research corpus (notes, transcripts, clips).
- An initial KPI / metrics list.
- A first cut of stakeholders and users.

## Exit criteria
You can articulate, in one paragraph, *who* has *what problem*, *in which context*, and *why now*. If you cannot, stay in Discover.
