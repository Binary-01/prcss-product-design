# 🧠 Ideate

**Color:** pink · **Emoji:** 🧠

## Intent
Generate many candidate solutions to the defined problem. This is the divergent phase of the second diamond — optimize for quantity and variety, not polish.

## Typical methods to add to this phase
- Crazy 8's
- Brainwriting / 6-3-5
- Design Studio
- Storyboarding
- Concept sketches
- "How Might We" ideation workshops
- Analogous inspiration sessions

## Typical outputs
- A wide set of concept sketches or written ideas.
- A first pass at concept selection (dot-voting, scoring).
- A shortlist of 2–4 directions worth prototyping.

## Exit criteria
You have enough distinct directions to make a real choice in the Design phase — not one obvious idea padded with three weak alternatives.
